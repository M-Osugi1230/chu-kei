# Chu-kei product principles

1. Chu-kei is a mid-term management plan information platform, not an investment-decision service.
2. The product helps users compare differences in corporate strategies.
3. Company count never overrides data quality.
4. Unknown information remains unknown.
5. Source evidence, verification state, and review state are visible.
6. Exploration coverage and structured analysis are never presented as the same thing.
7. AI may propose extraction candidates but may not publish production-quality data without human review.
8. Every paid feature must save meaningful research time or enable a workflow unavailable in the free experience.
9. Historical plan comparison and change tracking are treated as long-term proprietary data assets.
10. Revenue growth must not weaken neutrality, source transparency, or quality gates.
