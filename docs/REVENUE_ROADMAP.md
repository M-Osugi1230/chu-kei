# Revenue roadmap

## Stage 1: trust and usage

- complete v43 repository migration;
- improve quality visibility and strategy-first discovery;
- measure search, comparison, source-link, and return usage;
- interview target users.

Revenue target: none. Goal: evidence of repeated use.

## Stage 2: spot products

- theme-based verified company lists;
- industry comparison workbooks;
- custom screening and comparison reports.

Revenue target: first paid customer, then repeatable monthly spot revenue.

## Stage 3: Pro

- saved searches;
- watchlists;
- update and difference notifications;
- advanced comparison;
- CSV and Excel export;
- historical-plan comparison.

Revenue target: recurring individual subscriptions.

## Stage 4: Team

- shared lists and searches;
- team notes, tags, and assignments;
- organization administration;
- invoice and annual contracts.

Revenue target: recurring corporate contracts.

## Stage 5: API and licensed data

- company, plan, progress, source, and change APIs;
- customer-specific datasets;
- update SLAs and audit history.

Revenue target: high-value annual data contracts.

At every stage, product-quality and neutrality gates take precedence over short-term revenue.
