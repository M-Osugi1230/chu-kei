# Monetization guardrails

Chu-kei may charge for structured data, research-time reduction, exports, notifications, collaboration, historical comparisons, and API access.

Chu-kei must not charge for or present:

- a recommendation to purchase or sell a security;
- claims that a company is undervalued;
- claims that a user will profit;
- recommended stocks or rankings framed as investment attractiveness;
- success-rate or win-rate claims.

## Paid-value hierarchy

1. Verified structured data
2. Advanced search and comparison
3. Historical-plan differences
4. Change notifications and watchlists
5. CSV and Excel export
6. Team workflows
7. API and licensed datasets
8. Custom research

Quality state and source limitations remain visible in both free and paid plans.
