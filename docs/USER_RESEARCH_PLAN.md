# User research plan

## Priority segments

1. M&A advisory and intermediary professionals
2. Corporate planning and new-business teams
3. Consulting and research professionals
4. IR professionals
5. Individual corporate-research users

## Questions

- What task causes the user to open an IR document?
- How many companies must they inspect?
- Which comparison axes matter?
- How much time does the current workflow require?
- Which errors or omissions are unacceptable?
- Which output format is needed?
- Is the need recurring or one-time?
- What evidence would be required for paid use?
- What would the user pay for as a spot product?
- What would justify a monthly or annual contract?

## Evidence required before subscription development

- at least 20 completed interviews;
- at least five users who repeat the same workflow monthly;
- at least three users expressing concrete payment intent;
- at least one successfully sold spot product;
- clear separation between free discovery value and paid workflow value.
